## A Smile You're Proud To Show

Cosmetic dentistry at Smile Savers covers everything from a single whitening session to a complete smile transformation. Dr. Bhagat and Dr. Islam take time to understand what you want — then design a plan that gets you there.

### Teeth Whitening

Our in-office whitening uses professional-grade bleaching gel activated under a light — results in under an hour. We also offer take-home trays with custom-fitted moulds for gradual whitening on your schedule.

### Porcelain Veneers

Veneers are wafer-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front surface of teeth. They correct chips, cracks, gaps, and stubborn stains — and look entirely natural because they're matched precisely to your surrounding teeth.

### Smile Makeovers

A smile makeover combines two or more treatments — whitening, veneers, bonding, and sometimes orthodontics — into a single coordinated plan. We use digital smile design to show you a preview before any work begins.

### Dental Bonding

Tooth-coloured resin applied and sculpted directly onto the tooth. Ideal for small chips or gaps, completed in one visit, no lab required.
