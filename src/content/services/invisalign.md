## Straighter Teeth on Your Terms

Invisalign uses a series of clear, removable aligners custom-made for your teeth. Unlike metal braces, they're nearly invisible and fit around your lifestyle — not the other way around.

### The Invisalign Process at Smile Savers

**1. Digital Consultation**
We take 3D digital scans of your teeth — no impressions, no mess. You'll see a simulation of your projected result before committing to anything.

**2. Custom Aligners Made**
Your unique set of aligners is manufactured and shipped directly to us. Each tray is trimmed and polished for comfort.

**3. Wear & Progress**
You wear each set for 1–2 weeks, then move to the next. Check-ins every 6–8 weeks to track progress and hand over the next sets.

**4. Refinements & Retainers**
When treatment completes, we may take new scans for final refinement aligners. Retainers keep your result permanent.

### Who Is Invisalign For?

Invisalign works well for adults and teenagers with: crowded teeth, gaps between teeth, overbite, underbite, crossbite, and general misalignment. Schedule a free consultation to find out if you're a candidate.
