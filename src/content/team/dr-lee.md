Dr. Lee is a skilled practitioner who combines technical proficiency with a warm, caring approach. Patients appreciate his thorough explanations and gentle treatment style.

## Clinical Excellence

Dr. Lee's training at Stony Brook University provided a strong foundation in all aspects of general dentistry. He has since developed particular expertise in restorative procedures and endodontics.

## Treatment Philosophy

"I believe every patient deserves to fully understand their treatment options," says Dr. Lee. "My goal is to provide the information and care people need to make confident decisions about their dental health."

## Specializations

Dr. Lee is particularly skilled in:

- Complex restorative cases
- Root canal therapy
- Surgical and non-surgical extractions
- Emergency dental care

## Commitment to Patients

Whether treating a routine cavity or managing a dental emergency, Dr. Lee approaches every case with the same level of care and attention to detail. His calm presence helps patients feel at ease even during challenging procedures.
